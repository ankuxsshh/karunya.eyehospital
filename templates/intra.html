Intravitreal Services

Our retina specialists treat retinal conditions like Diabetic Retinopathy, Retinal Vein Occlusion (RVO), Age-related Macular Degeneration (AMD), Polypoidal Choroidal Vasculopathy (PCV), and other Cytomegalovirus (CMVs).

We provide a variety of Anti-VEGF injections such as:

  • Injection Razumab (Ranibizumab - 2.3mg)
  • Injection Accentrix (Ranibizumab - 10mg/ml)
  • Injection Pagenax (Brolucizumab - 120mg/ml)
  • Injection Eylea (Aflibercept - 40mg/ml)
