Medical Retinal Service

KEH specializes in treating retinal and choroidal diseases, including Diabetic retinopathy, Age-related macular degeneration (AMD), and Retinal vascular diseases. Our dedicated team provides comprehensive Hospital for complex conditions, including those affecting children and individuals with hereditary disorders.

KEH, renowned for its medical retinal services, provides specialized Hospital for various retinal and choroidal diseases. These include diabetic retinopathy, hypertensive retinopathy, and retinal vascular occlusions, which are often associated with systemic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, cardiac issues, high cholesterol, and anaemia.

Our expertise extends to treating acquired retinal diseases, such as retinal detachments and holes, infections, and non-infectious diseases affecting the retina and choroid. Fathima Eye Hospital is also dedicated to managing retinal diseases in children and those caused by blood disorders, drug toxicities, and genetic conditions.

We offer comprehensive screening packages for various medical retinal diseases. Diabetic patients should have annual eye screenings to detect diabetic retinopathy. If you haven’t had a dilated eye exam in the last year, it’s time to schedule one. Our advanced dyeless OCT Angiography and Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) help detect early signs of diabetic retinopathy and macular degeneration.

Additionally, we provide Drug Toxicity Screening, ROP Screening for premature babies, and genetic counselling for families with hereditary eye diseases, ensuring a tailored and complete approach to retinal Hospital.
