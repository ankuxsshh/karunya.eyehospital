Nursing Superintendent

Working with Karunya since 2016. Oversee and coordinate all nursing staff and patient care activities... ensuring the highest standards of ophthalmic nursing care are consistently maintained. Ensure accurate implementation of clinical protocols, particularly for pre & post operative eye care, infection control, and medication administration. Organize regular training sessions to keep nursing staff updated on the latest ophthalmic procedures, nursing practices, and patient safety standards. Monitor nursing practices and outcomes to maintain compliance with NABH standards relevant to eye care. Work closely with ophthalmologists and allied health professionals to ensure seamless patient care, addressing individual needs and enhancing patient satisfaction. Manage nursing schedules, optimize staff allocation, and ensure availability of necessary supplies and equipment within the nursing units. Develop, implement, and periodically review nursing policies and standard operating procedures tailored to the specific needs of ophthalmic patients. Ensure accurate and timely documentation of nursing activities, patient care plans, and incident reports, maintaining confidentiality and data integrity.
