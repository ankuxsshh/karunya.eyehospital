Management at Karunya Eye Hospital

Our Management Team

Meet the dedicated professionals who lead our hospital with hospitality and excellence.

Mr. Tilly B George

Chairman

He has been successful in all his efforts to grow Karunya Eye Hospital as one of the best Eye Hospitals in Kerala. Being the Chairman & Managing Director of the Hospital, he aims for a system that is process driven and immaculate, which follows strict protocol and rules. The tradition of “committed to excellent eye care” continues from the time the hospital started 18 years ago, thanks to the meticulous system initiated by his family. His knowledge in the field of Opticals and Eye Clinic, has enabled him to relate to doctors and paramedics working in the hospital. Before taking on the reins of administration at Karunya Eye Hospital, he had extended his knowledge for the growth of K.K Opticals & Eye Clinic at Adoor, which he has started around 40 years ago. Following the tradition of quality treatment at affordable cost, he upholds the mantra of the hospital “committed to excellent eye care”.

Mr. Thomas John

Chief Operating Officer

Mr. Thomas John joined with Karunya Eye Hospital in July 2017 and his contributions helped the Hospital to reach in its present position. He is the Chief Operating Officer responsible for monitoring the strictness in the operational function of the hospital. By pioneering various strategies of administration for upgrading the internal quality, his admiring service for the past eight years has tagged him with the identity as the strongest pillar of KEH. He plays a major role in determining the organisation’s operations, human resources, empanelment with Government Projects like ECHS, CGHS, MEDISEP and major Private Health Insurance Companies in India and accreditation process with NABH. He is actively involved in the day-to-day activities of the organisation. No matter how busy, he ensures that he finds time to personally mentor staff and help them hone their skills. He has around 35 years of experience and prior to joining Karunya, he had worked with reputed Companies in India and abroad.

Mr. Suresh Kumar

Centre Head Kottarakara

Having Twenty Six years of experience with Army Medical Corps (OT Manager) and Ten Years of experience in reputed Eye Hospitals in Kerala in the position as OT Manager and Centre Head. Presently he is the Team Leader & Centre Head of our Kottarakkara Centre.

Mr. Mukesh Kumar

Centre Head Pandalam

Mr. Mukhesh drives the customer relations of Karunya Eye Hospital, and ensures that a healthy relationship is maintained between the hospital and its patients/customers. He is working with Karunya for the last seven years and prior to joining Karunya, he has worked in different positions in reputed Private Organizations in India as well as abroad over 27 years. Passionate about people and building relations, he is capable of handling difficult situations without losing his cool and addresses any dispute the public has with the hospital. He is now the Centre Head & Team Leader of our Pandalam Centre.
