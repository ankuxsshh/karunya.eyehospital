About Karunya Eye Hospital

Established in 1985 as K.K. Opticals & Eye Clinic in Adoor, Kerala, by Mr. Tilly B George, a pioneer in optical lenses and accessories, our institution has grown remarkably over 40 years. His vision of a premier eye care center became a reality with Karunya Eye Hospital (KEH) in 2008. KEH, a NABH-certified specialty eye care hospital, is one of the most trusted in Adoor, with modern centers in Pandalam and Kottarakkara. Our eight highly qualified specialists provide advanced ophthalmic care, including cataract, retina, glaucoma, and laser eye surgeries. Thousands of painless MICS cataract surgeries (Phaco) have been successfully performed. Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced specialists, and certified technicians ensure superior eye care. Conveniently located, KEH is 95 km from Thiruvananthapuram International Airport and 26 km from the nearest railway station. We remain committed to providing quality eye care with continual advancements in technology and expertise.
